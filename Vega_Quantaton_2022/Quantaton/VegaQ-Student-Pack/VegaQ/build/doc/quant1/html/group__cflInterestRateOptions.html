Computes the futures price of the cheapest bond to deliver. The set of times, when the futures price is evaluated is given by

\[ t_i = t_0 + i\frac{T-t_0}{M}, \quad i=1,\dots,M, \]

where \(t_0=\) rModel.initialTime(), \(T=\) dFuturesMaturity, and \(M=\) iFuturesTimes. At the maturity, the seller of the contract can deliver any bond from rBonds.

Parameters
dFuturesMaturityThe maturity of the futures contract.
iFuturesTimesThe number of futures times, that is, the times when the futures price is determined.
rBondsThe parameters of the underlying bonds. We assume that the issue times for all bonds equal dFuturesMaturity.
rModelThe reference to the implementation of cfl::InterestRateModel.
Returns
The price of the option as the function of the initial values of the state processes in the model.

Computes the price of puttable and callable coupon bond. In this contract, at any coupon time less than maturity the following events take place in their respective order:

  1. The coupon payment.
  2. Holder of the bond has the right to sell the bond to the issuer. In this case, the holder receives the redemption amount rCouponBond.notional * dRedemptionPrice and the bond is terminated.
  3. Issuer of the bond has the right to buy the bond back. In this case, he pays the repurchase amount rCouponBond.notional * dRepurchasePrice and the bond is terminated.
Parameters
rCouponBondThe parameters of the underlying coupon bond. The issue time for the bond coincides with the initial time of the model.
dRedemptionPriceThe redemption price of the bond given as a percentage of the notional. After the coupon payment, the holder of the bond can sell it back to the issuer for the amount rCouponBond.notional * dRedemptionPrice. Typically, this amount is less than the notional.
dRepurchasePriceThe repurchase price of the bond given as a percentage of the notional. After the coupon payment, the issuer of the bond can buy the bond back from the holder for the amount rCouponBond.notional * dRepurchasePrice. Usually, this amount is greater than the notional.
rModelReference to implementation of cfl::InterestRateModel.
Returns
The price of the option as the function of the initial values of the state processes in the model.
