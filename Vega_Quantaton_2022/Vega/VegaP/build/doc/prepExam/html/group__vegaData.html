Computes forward curve

\[ F(t) = S_0 e^{q(t)(t-t_0)}, \quad t\in [t_0,t_M], \]

where cost-of-carry rate \(q=q(t)\) is obtained by the linear interpolation of the market cost-of-carry rates. On the first interval \([t_0,t_1]\), the cost-of-carry rate is constant:

\[ q(t) = q(t_1), \quad t\in [t_0,t_1]. \]

Parameters
dSpot\(S_0\) The spot price of the stock.
rDeliveryTimes\((t_i)_{i=1,\dots,M}\) The maturities of the market forward contracts, \(t_0<t_1\).
rForwardPrices\((F(t_i))_{i=1,\dots,M}\) The market forward prices.
dInitialTime\(t_0\) The initial time.
Returns
The forward curve obtained by the linear interpolation of the market cost-of-carry rates.

std::function<double(double)> vega::forwardLibor ( double  dLiborPeriod,
const std::function< double(double)> &  rDiscount

Computes the curve of forward LIBORs. It costs nothing to enter the forward rate agreement (FRA) with maturity \(t\) and period \(\delta t\) as either a borrower or a lender. Forward LIBOR \(L^f(t,t+\delta t)\) is set at issue time \(t_0\). In the contract,

  1. At maturity \(t\), the borrower receives notional \(N\).
  2. At time \(t+\delta t\), the borrower pays the notional plus the interest computed at the forward LIBOR:

    \[ N(1+ L^f(t,t+\delta t)\delta t). \]

Parameters
dLiborPeriod\(\delta t\) The LIBOR period.
rDiscountThe discount curve.
Returns
\((L^f(t,t+\delta t))_{t\geq t_0}\) The curve of forward LIBORs.

Computes yield curve \(\gamma=\gamma(t)\) for the Svensson model:

\[ \gamma(t) = c_0 + c_1 \frac{1-e^{-\lambda_1(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_1 (t-t_0)} + c_2 \left(\frac{1-e^{-\lambda_1(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_1 (t-t_0)} - e^{-\lambda_1 (t-t_0)}\right) + c_3 \left(\frac{1-e^{-\lambda_2(t-t_0)}}{\lambda_2 (t-t_0)} - e^{-\lambda_2 (t-t_0)}\right) , \quad t\geq t_0. \]

Parameters
dC0\(c_0\) The first constant
dC1\(c_1\) The second constant.
dC2\(c_2\) The third constant.
dC3\(c_3\) The fourth constant.
dLambda1\(\lambda_1>0\) The first mean-reversion rate.
dLambda2\(\lambda_2>0\) The second mean-reversion rate, \(\lambda_1\not=\lambda_2\).
dInitialTime\(t_0\) The initial time.
Returns
The yield curve for the Svensson model.
